# Admin User Guide

## Navigation
Use the **sidebar** to access:
- **Inventory** — View and manage stock levels
- **Log Entry** — Submit logs on behalf of staff
- **Transfers** — Move stock between branches
- **Staff** — Manage team members
- **Analytics** — View consumption reports
- **Audit Log** — Track all changes

## Inventory Management

### Viewing Stock
- Switch between branches using the tabs at the top
- Use the **category dropdown** to filter by Powder, Liquid, or Addon
- Click column headers to **sort** by name, stock levels, etc.

### Adding Ingredients
1. Click **Add Item**
2. Enter ingredient name and starting stock
3. Select a category
4. Click **Add Item**

### Editing Ingredients
1. Hover over a row
2. Click the **pencil icon**
3. Update name, category, or starting stock
4. Click **Save**

### Physical Count
1. Click **Enter count** under Physical Count column
2. Enter the actual count
3. Click **Save**
4. Variance is shown automatically (green = surplus, red = shortage)

### Deleting Ingredients
1. Hover over a row
2. Click the **trash icon**
3. Confirm deletion (logs for that item are also removed)

## Transfers
1. Go to **Transfers**
2. Select **source** and **destination** branches
3. Select the ingredient and quantity
4. Click **Transfer**
5. Both branches' stock is updated automatically

## Analytics
- **Summary cards** show key metrics
- **Consumption chart** shows usage over time
- **Variance report** compares expected vs actual
- Click **Export CSV** to download the report

## Staff Management
1. Go to **Staff**
2. Click **Invite Staff**
3. Enter email and assign a branch
4. The staff member receives an email invitation

To change a staff member's branch, click the branch badge next to their name.

## Dark Mode
Click the sun/moon icon in the header to toggle dark mode. Your preference is saved.
